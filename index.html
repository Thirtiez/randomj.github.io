About Me

I am a skilled software developer with a strong passion for videogames.
I am open minded, accepting and casual. You can count on me to help keep a relaxed environment: I am patient event in the most stressful situations, whether I'm working or playing.

Whenever I code, robustness, scalability and clarity are always my priorities. I believe coding patterns and standards are there for a reason, and I never reinvent the wheel.
I always do my best to ensure clear communication and absolute reliability whenever I work in a team, and I learned how to effectively collaborate with artists, designers and other developers.

During my free time, beside playing (a lot of) videogames, I also love to hang out with friends and play tabletop games, RPGs and escape rooms.
I watch movies, tv series, I read comics and books; sports may not be my thing, but I sure love good stories.
I also work on personal projects, because why not? I enjoy making games.

Since I was a kid, I wanted to become a game developer. I have now reached that goal, but I won't stop here.
I am ambitious and driven, I thrive challenge and I'm always looking for an opportunity to grow.

Stars - Thesis project for Altec SPA

I developed from scratch a VR application for the realistic visualization and navigation of astronomical data.

It is based on a huge dataset from the Hipparcus catalogue (100000+ stars) that will eventually be extended to the Gaia catalogue (billions of stars). The application has precise scale and distances, from kilometers to parsecs.

Developed with Unity as Standalone application.
