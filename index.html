About me

I am a skilled software developer, with a strong passion for videogames.

Enigma

University project.

The game is a first-person puzzle game situated in a small world designed by putting together some of the most popular paintings by Giorgio De Chirico.
The goal of the game is to solve puzzles hidden among the elements of the scene and unlock descriptions of the paintings related to the section you are visiting.

Developed in C# with Unity3D for WebGL, Blender as modeling tool.

Stars

Thesis project.

I collaborated with an Aerospace company to develop a VR application for the visualization and navigation of astronomical data.
The data was taken from the Hipparcus catalogue (100000+ stars) and will eventually be extended to the Gaia catalogue (billions of stars). The application has realistic and precise scale and distances.

Developed in C# with Unity3D as standalone Windows application.

DICOM HoloViewer
(health company)

Work project.

It's a Hololens 1 & 2 application for the visualization and analysis of biometric data.
First it creates a 3D voxel model from a set of DICOM images, then you can visualize it and interact with it through Hololens gestures.
You can work with the colorization to highlight important bits and cut sections of the model to reveal what's inside.

Developed in C# with Unity3D as UWP application.

Gamification of selling plan
(fashion group)

Work project.

Due to the global pandemic and the related lockdown, a client in the fashion sector wanted to completely digitalize the process of presentation and planning of a new collection.
The planning part was gamified in a 'casino' way in which an area manager could bet for certain products as an indication of purchase.
This application is heavily data-driven and was integrated in a e-commerce site.

Front-end developed in C# with Unity3D as WebGL application.
Back-end developed in C# with .NET Core as a WebAPI application communicating with a SQL database.
